Programs

A Common Future’s programs respond to the urgent need to ensure that digital transformation advances human rights, inclusion, and public interest. Our work bridges community realities with global digital and governance agendas.

Human Rights & Civic Space Protection

We promote awareness, accountability, and access to rights, particularly in vulnerable and underserved communities.

Relevance for donors: Supports Sustainable Development Goals on peace, justice, and strong institutions.

Digital Rights & Internet Freedom

We advocate for privacy, freedom of expression, and safe digital environments, enabling individuals and communities to participate freely and securely online.

Relevance for donors: Aligns with global priorities on digital democracy and civic resilience.

Internet as a Public Good

We promote affordable, equitable access and inclusive connectivity to ensure that the internet serves as a shared resource for social and economic development.

Relevance for donors: Contributes directly to digital inclusion and inclusive development outcomes.

Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)

We advance rights-respecting approaches to digital identification systems, platforms, and public services that prioritize people over profit or control.

Relevance for donors: Supports people-centered digital public infrastructure and safeguards against exclusion and misuse.

AI Literacy & Responsible Technology

We build public understanding of artificial intelligence and emerging technologies, empowering communities to engage critically with technological change.

Relevance for donors: Responds to the growing global need for ethical, transparent, and inclusive AI ecosystems.

Research, Education & Sensitization

We generate local knowledge, conduct research, and strengthen community capacity through education and public awareness initiatives.

Relevance for donors: Enables evidence-based programming and informed policy engagement.

Human Rights & Internet Freedom Festivals

We use arts, culture, and creative expression to expand civic dialogue, foster youth engagement, and build inclusive digital cultures.

Relevance for donors: An innovative civic education and social cohesion tool that reaches diverse audiences.
