Our Programs

A Common Future designs and implements programs that advance human rights, digital inclusion, and people-centered technology. Our work responds to local realities while engaging with regional and global policy processes.

Human Rights & Civic Space Protection

We promote awareness, accountability, and access to rights, particularly among vulnerable and underserved communities. Our work strengthens civic participation, protects fundamental freedoms, and supports human rights defenders operating in fragile contexts.

Relevance for donors: Supports SDGs on peace, justice, and strong institutions, and contributes to democratic resilience.

Digital Rights & Internet Freedom

We advocate for privacy, freedom of expression, and safe digital environments, addressing online harms, surveillance risks, and restrictions on civic participation in digital spaces.

Relevance for donors: Aligns with global priorities on digital democracy and civic resilience.

Internet as a Public Good

We promote the internet as a public good that must remain open, affordable, and accessible to all, advancing equitable connectivity and challenging digital exclusion.

Relevance for donors: Contributes to digital inclusion and inclusive development outcomes.

Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)

We advance rights-respecting approaches to digital public infrastructure, including digital identification systems, platforms, and public services, with a focus on accountability and inclusion.

Relevance for donors: Supports people-centered DPI and safeguards against exclusion and misuse.

AI Literacy & Responsible Technology

We build public understanding of artificial intelligence and emerging technologies, enabling communities, youth, and civil society actors to engage critically with technological change.

Relevance for donors: Addresses the growing need for ethical and inclusive AI ecosystems.

Research, Education & Sensitization

We generate local evidence, conduct applied research, and deliver education and sensitization initiatives that strengthen community capacity and inform policy debates.

Relevance for donors: Enables evidence-based programming and policy engagement.

Human Rights & Internet Freedom Festivals

We use arts, culture, and creative expression to expand civic dialogue, amplify youth voices, and make human rights and digital issues accessible to wider audiences.
