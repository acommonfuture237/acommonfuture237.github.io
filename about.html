About Us

Who We Are

A Common Future (ACF) is an independent, non-profit organization working to ensure that digital transformation advances justice, inclusion, and human dignity.

Our work bridges local realities and global policy spaces, contributing grounded perspectives to international conversations on:

  • Digital rights
  • Internet governance
  • Digital public infrastructure
  • Artificial intelligence and emerging technologies
  • Rights-based development and humanitarian action

We serve as a connector and convener, linking communities, policymakers, technologists, and development partners to shape digital futures that are inclusive, accountable, and people-centered.

Our Vision

We envision a world where:

  • Human rights are fully protected online and offline
  • Digital spaces are open, safe, and inclusive
  • Public-interest technology strengthens democracy and equity
  • Marginalized communities shape digital futures, not just experience them

Our Mission

To transform lives and build common frontiers by advancing human rights and digital inclusion through advocacy, research, education, and multi-stakeholder engagement.

Our Approach

Rights-Based Framework

We integrate international human rights principles into digital policy, development work, and humanitarian action.

Community-Centered Solutions

We co-create programs with communities, ensuring that solutions respond to lived realities rather than imposed external models.

Multi-Stakeholder Engagement

We foster dialogue and collaboration among civil society, government, academia, the private sector, and affected communities.

Evidence-Informed Advocacy

Our research, learning, and field insights inform advocacy, policy reform, and institutional practice at local, national, and global levels.
