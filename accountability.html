Accountability & Transparency

A Common Future is committed to high standards of accountability, transparency, and ethical practice. These principles guide our governance, partnerships, and program implementation.

Our Commitment

ACF upholds accountability through the following core practices:

  • Responsible financial stewardship, ensuring funds are used efficiently and for their intended purposes
  • Monitoring, evaluation, and learning (MEL) to assess outcomes, strengthen impact, and inform continuous improvement
  • Transparent and accountable partnerships, based on clarity of roles, responsibilities, and shared objectives
  • Ethical, community-centered programming that respects dignity, participation, and local ownership

Reporting and Compliance

We provide narrative and financial reporting aligned with donor requirements and international good practice. Our reporting supports:

  • Clear documentation of activities and outcomes
  • Financial accountability and compliance
  • Learning, adaptation, and evidence-based decision-making

Through these practices, A Common Future ensures that resources entrusted to us generate measurable impact, institutional integrity, and public trust.
