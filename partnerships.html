Partnerships

A Common Future builds strategic, values-driven partnerships to advance human rights, digital inclusion, and people-centered technology. We believe meaningful impact is achieved through collaboration, shared learning, and mutual accountability.

Who We Work With

We collaborate with a diverse range of partners, including:

  • International foundations supporting rights-based and inclusive development
  • Development agencies working across governance, technology, and social justice
  • Academic and research institutions generating evidence and policy insights
  • Digital rights and civic technology networks advancing open and accountable digital spaces
  • Technology and policy organizations shaping digital governance and public-interest innovation

How We Partner

  • Shared values and principles, particularly human rights and inclusion
  • Learning and knowledge exchange across local, national, and global contexts
  • Co-creation of programs and initiatives that respond to real-world needs
  • Impact-oriented collaboration with clear outcomes and accountability

We welcome partnerships that are committed to long-term impact, respect local leadership, and contribute to inclusive digital futures.
